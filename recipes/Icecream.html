Protein Ice Cream

A homemade Protein Ice Cream

I discovered this recipe from the cookbook of Greg Doucette (The Anabolic Cookbook 2.0)

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder (Chocolate flavored)
  • Xanthum gum
  • PB2 Powder
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Sweetner (Fake sugar)

Steps

  1. Start by putting ice into blender and grinding it down
  2. Follow this up by adding around half a cup of milk of your choice (Almond works best here)
  3. Blend that together and then add a full serving of your protein powder
  4. Add a serving of your PB2 powder (2 TBSPS)
  5. Blend that all together, It should be liquid at this point.
  6. Add in around 2 teaspoons of xanthum gum and 2 packets of your sweetner
  7. The xanthum gum will severely thicken this dish into making it the (Ice Cream) that it's supposed to be
  8. Once all blended together you're going to want to bowl it into a chilled bowl and eat
