Chicken Parm Meal

Chicken parmasean and spaghetti noodles

A classic italian chicken parmasean recipe feautring spaghetti and marinara with sprinkles of basil and fried. This is an excellent dish for a weeknight meal and can be made in a pinch. I often make this for my family or my girlfriend and they love it!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Shredded Mozzarella and Parmgiano Regiano
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Breading Ingredients (Flour, Egg and Panko)
  • Ingredients for spaghetti (Noodles, Jarred sauce or home made!)
  • Sprigs of basil

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling a package of spaghetti noodles and putting your jarred sauce in a pot on the stove to simmer
  2. Clean your chicken breasts and butterfly them, then tenderizing them
  3. Begin to heat a cast iron skillet of vegetable oil, Enough to submerge a piece of chicken
  4. In 3 seperate bowls put Panko, Whisked eggs, and Flour
  5. First dip a piece of your chicken into the flour, then the eggs, And finally the Panko.
  6. Once it has been coated in the 3 breading ingredients proceed to drop it into the skillet of oil
  7. Wait around 5 minutes and then flip the piece of chicken, Proceed to cook it another few minutes until it internally reaches 165 degrees Farenheit
  8. Drain your spaghetti noodles and set aside.
  9. Take your chicken out of the skillet and put on a plate, Pour shredded Mozzarella and Parmgiano Regiano over to melt
  10. Put Noodles onto side of plate and pour over simmered sauce,
  11. Finish the dish by shredding basil onto dish and voila!
